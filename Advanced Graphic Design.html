What is Graphic Design?

Graphic design can be best defined as the art of combining text & pictures to create a visual design, which would be used in the print and publishing field. Graphic design is something which drives advertising and attracts us to brands.



What is Motion Graphics?


Motion graphics design is the art of making things or texts move in a video. This is used in movies to show the title and end credits, and marketers use it to make videos to promote their products and brands. To put it simply, it is graphics that move.


Motion graphics are used by almost every company that spends money on marketing, as they are an important part of their promotional videos.
